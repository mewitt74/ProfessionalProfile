Policy Exception Program (PXR)

SOX Control Framework Redesign

×

Situation

  • Company A's Policy Exception (PXR) program supported SOX & regulatory frameworks
  • Volume growing 46% year-over-year
  • Legacy workflow = slow, manual, hard to audit

Challenge

  • Make decisions faster and risk-based
  • Generate clean audit evidence automatically
  • Satisfy Finance, Internal Audit, and Engineering stakeholders

Actions Taken

  • Mapped end-to-end process with Finance, Internal Audit, Engineering
  • Identified risk decisions happening informally (email vs system)
  • Defined standardized control patterns with required approvers by risk tier
  • Partnered with Engineering to embed rules into PXR tooling
  • Worked with Finance & Audit to validate SOX assertions

Controls Monitoring Framework

Cross-Functional Control Design

×

Situation

  • Multiple control frameworks operated independently (Finance, IT, Compliance)
  • Policy translated differently by each group
  • Overlapping, duplicative testing consuming resources

Challenge

  • Unify 310+ controls across all frameworks
  • Translate policy into technical properties
  • Satisfy Finance assurance, Audit evidence, and Engineering automation simultaneously

Actions Taken

  • Designed shared control catalog with unified definitions
  • Translated policy language into technical properties: signals, thresholds, cadences
  • Created feedback loops so Finance, Audit, and Engineering could validate design
  • Embedded controls into shared monitoring platform
