STAR Stories: Compliance Impact in Action

1
Automating SOX Evidence Collection
Situation
Challenge: Manual evidence gathering for SOX controls was time-consuming and error-prone.
Task
Goal: Design a scalable, automated solution to collect and store audit evidence.
Action
Solution: Developed automated workflows integrated with engineering pipelines, ensuring evidence was captured at the source and stored securely.
Result
Impact: Reduced audit prep time by 40%, improved accuracy, and enabled real-time compliance monitoring.
This transformation enabled teams to focus on value-added analysis rather than manual documentation, while auditors gained confidence in the integrity and completeness of evidence.
2
Scaling ISO 27001 Controls
Situation
Challenge: Rapid company growth outpaced manual control processes, risking ISO 27001 certification.
Task
Goal: Embed controls into engineering workflows to ensure continuous compliance at scale.
Action
Solution: Partnered with engineering to automate control checks in CI/CD pipelines and implemented real-time dashboards for control health.
Result
Impact: Maintained ISO 27001 certification through two audits, with zero major findings and 30% less manual effort.
The approach ensured compliance was "silent" and embedded, supporting rapid scaling without sacrificing control rigor or audit readiness.
3
NIST Framework Modernization
Situation
Challenge: Legacy NIST controls were fragmented and difficult to test, leading to audit delays.
Task
Goal: Unify and modernize NIST controls for automation and continuous monitoring.
Action
Solution: Mapped regulatory requirements to technical signals, automated evidence collection, and created a shared control catalog.
Result
Impact: Reduced audit cycle time by 25% and improved control pass rate to 97%.
The unified framework enabled real-time visibility for stakeholders and streamlined audit processes, supporting ongoing compliance.
4
AI Governance for Emerging Risks
Situation
Challenge: Emerging AI use cases introduced new compliance and ethical risks.
Task
Goal: Establish a governance framework for responsible AI development and deployment.
Action
Solution: Developed policies, risk assessments, and technical controls for AI systems, ensuring transparency and accountability.
Result
Impact: Enabled safe AI adoption, passed external audits, and built stakeholder trust in AI initiatives.
The governance model positioned the company as a leader in responsible AI, supporting innovation while managing risk.
